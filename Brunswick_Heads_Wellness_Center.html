Brunswick Heads Wellness Center

Site & Social Background

Brunswick Heads is a small, coastal village on the NSW north coast. Brunswick Head has al strong community culture. There are lots of sports-related community groups such as Triathlon, Surfing, Softball, and tennis. It is worth noting, that most of these sports are not suitable for the orders in this town. In an aging society, the Middle-aged and elderly people also created community groups to build relationships with each other.

Key Concept

Humans are not free-standing isolated individuals, we are all members of each other. Life’s simple pleasures come from lasting meaningful relationships with others such as family, friends, and communities. People suffice to live in a well-grounded community with a common culture and moral infrastructure.

The design vision for the Brunswick Heads Wellness Center is to create a space for a walking club in Brunswick Heads. Walking is a super-inclusive exercise that everyone can participate in. Walking exercise does not require skills and has a low impact on the body. This democratic form of body practice and exercise build culturally on Brunswick Heads’ old people’s condition. The reality of Brunswick Heads and its condition does not allow collectivity of body wellness practice to exist. The aging people in rural Australia find themselves lost of community and lost of access to sporting club activities. The wellness center will reinforce a community culture of mutual support and caring, enhance their well-being and improve their quality of life. The elders will be encouraged to take collective activities and integrate with different age groups more meaningfully and socially.

Typology

This project is a hybrid public wellness center. At its facilities, the residents, especially the elders are offered a platform for a Walking Club as well as a variety of recreational activities and wellness practices such as Yoga, collective dancing and sports installations.
