About Me

I am Jinhao Lu, an architect who graduated from the University of Melbourne in Bachelor of Architecture. I am self-driven and willing to dabble in different fields. I have accumulated various architecture types, knowledge, and design experience. For example library, collective housing, terraced house, parametric design, and landscape design. I am skilled in multiple design software including Rhino, Enscape, Adobe, CAD, etc. I believe in the power of holistically good ideas paired with logic derivation. I am passionate about humanistic architecture that also connects to the environment.
